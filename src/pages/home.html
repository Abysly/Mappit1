Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Event Mapper

Is Event Mapper free to use?

Yes! Event Mapper offers a free tier with access to most public events. Our premium membership unlocks additional features like exclusive events, advanced analytics, and priority support.

How do I add my own events?

After creating an account, you can submit events through our organizer dashboard. All events go through a quick verification process before going live on the platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay. All transactions are securely processed with encryption.

Can I get a refund for premium membership?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all premium memberships. After 30 days, we can prorate refunds for the remaining time on a case-by-case basis.
